CV Syafa Web Administration

In this project, I worked as a full-stack web developer to develop a financial administration website for the company CV Syafa Water, located in Ciamis, West Java. This website enables the company's financial administrator to monitor the stock of goods and the financial status of the company. In developing this website, I used PHP for the backend, and HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the frontend.
