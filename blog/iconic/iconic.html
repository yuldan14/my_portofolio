ICONIC IT 2024

On Friday, September 27, 2024, I served as the chief organizer for a program run by the Informatics Student Association of Siliwangi University. The ICONIC IT 2024 (Innovative Competition and National Informatics Conference) program was organized with the aim of providing a platform for competition and skill development in the field of information technology for students across Indonesia. This event consisted of two main activities: the IT Competition, which has already taken place and was participated in by students from public and private universities throughout Indonesia, and the National Seminar with the theme "Designing Superior Strategies to Face the Wave of Job Transformation for Programmers in the Era of Artificial Intelligence."

The IT Competition of ICONIC IT 2024 consists of three competition categories, namely:

  1. The data science competition was participated in by 53 teams.
  2. The web development competition was participated in by 40 teams.
  3. And the UI/UX Design Competition was participated in by 49 teams.

Overall, this competition was participated in by a total of 142 teams from 60 public and private universities across Indonesia.

In addition to the competition, this event also included a National Seminar featuring experts and practitioners in the fields of artificial intelligence (AI) and information technology. The seminar provided in-depth insights into the significant changes in the job market, particularly for programming professions, and equipped participants with knowledge on strategies to adapt to rapid technological advancements to enhance competitiveness in this era of digital transformation.

The invited speakers included:

  1. Dr. Ayi Purbasari, S.T., M.T. (Keynote Speaker, Vice Chairman of APTIKOM West Java)
  2. Prof. Yusep Rosmansyah, S.T., M.Sc., Ph.D. (Lecturer at Bandung Institute of Technology)
  3. Herman Sugi Harto, S.Kom. (AI/ML & Full Stack Engineer)
