1. Zetro: Learning Gold (December 2022)

Zetro is my first group-project at a College which was written by three members and one lecturer. This project is in the form of a website that can predict the value of gold based on data. The method of predict that we use is time-series forecasting. Based on the definition of Time-series Forecasting that i found on Tableau, Time series forecasting is the process of analyzing time series data using statistics and modeling to make predictions and inform strategic decision-making. It's not always an exact prediction, and likelihood of forecasts can vary wildly—especially when dealing with the commonly fluctuating variables in time series data as well as factors outside our control. However, forecasting insight about which outcomes are more likely—or less likely—to occur than other potential outcomes. Often, the more comprehensive the data we have, the more accurate the forecasts can be. While forecasting and “prediction” generally mean the same thing, there is a notable distinction. In some industries, forecasting might refer to data at a specific future point in time, while prediction refers to future data in general. Series forecasting is often used in conjunction with time series analysis. Time series analysis involves developing models to gain an understanding of the data to understand the underlying causes. Analysis can provide the “why” behind the outcomes you are seeing. Forecasting then takes the next step of what to do with that knowledge and the predictable extrapolations of what might happen in the future.

This journal is written based on monetary crisis which went viral on 2022. Furthermore, Corona pandemics was still existed on 2022. It has a huge impact on economic viability and stability. Zetro is a software that can help predict the value of gold which is never increased-decreased significantly. It may helpful for investor to invest their money into gold and know when they can sell the gold so that they get profit.

2. Data Mining: Prediction of Priority Food Commodities in Indonesia's New Capital (IKN) (June 2023)

This journal was written based on the issue of Indonesian capital relocation. The relocation of the Capital of Indonesia has a huge impact in various aspects. One of them is economic's aspect. There are lots of things to prepare. Food security is one of several things to watch out for. This resarch is useful to predict the productivity of agricultural and livestock product from 2021 until 2025. Based on data, we can predict the productivity using ARIMA algorythm. On the other hand, we also do clustering of the commodities into some categories from priority until not priority.
